Model Formulas and Assumptions

Formulas Used

EOQ Formula (Economic Order Quantity)
Q*  =  √ ( 2 × Dannual × S ) / hannual 
Reorder Point Formula
ROP  =  d̄ × L  +  z × σd × √ L 
Safety Stock Formula
SS  =  z × σd × √ L 
Service Level Formula
SL  =  (cycles without stockout) ÷ (total cycles)
Fill Rate Formula
FR  =  (total demand satisfied) ÷ (total demand generated)
Total Cost Reference Formula
TCannual  =  (S × Dannual / Q)  +  (hannual × Q / 2)  +  (π × annual lost units)

Model Assumptions

  • Continuous review inventory system: inventory position is evaluated every week
  • Lead time is constant and deterministic with no variability
  • No backorders: all unmet demand in a given week is permanently lost
  • Holding cost is linear and proportional to the number of units held each week
  • Orders are always of fixed size Q, placed immediately when inventory hits or falls below ROP
  • Only one order can be outstanding at a time (no order is placed if one is already in transit)
  • Single product, single echelon supply chain with no supply constraints
  • No minimum order quantities or maximum storage capacity constraints
  • Demand is statistically independent across weeks
  • Initial inventory is set to Q + ROP to avoid distorted results in the early weeks of the simulation
